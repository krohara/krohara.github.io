Meet the Foodie

Hi and Welcome! I’m Kelsey, and I’m the foodie behind this blog. This is where I write, cook, and post about delicious food. Currently, I’m a university student who studies by day and blogs at night. I think you can imagine my sleep schedule...

I’m a food enthusiast with an appetite for almost anything. I love to bake desserts and cook comfort-styled meals, but I’m not strict about what I eat. I will try anything from spicy calamari to pad thai. Any and all food is welcome!

On this blog you will find: reviews, recipes, photos (aka food porn), and daily posting discussing any topics relating to food. I will be experimenting with videos by recording my recipes, tasting new restaurants in Wisconsin, and interview local chefs about their process and how they create their dishes. Let me know if you want to learn more about me, check out my Facebook page or my Instagram.

Passion for Food

I think it’s difficult to find someone who doesn’t love food. Whether it’s cooking, baking, or just eating, everyone can find something enjoyable in food. For me, my passion started through my mom. She wasn’t the world’s best chef, but she would always tried to incorporate me in the kitchen from a young age.

I was ten years old when I made my first full meal for my family (even though it was pasta noodles with butter and steamed broccoli), and ever since then, I have strived to get creative in the kitchen and cook more than ever.
